Hanoi paint is a long-standing brand, with 60 years of experience in this field. However, the image of the brand gradually became outdated and did not reach the majority of consumers. That is our challenge in this project, we need to breathe new life into the brand from the image to the approach to the content.

There are 3 study programs, the names of the programs are inspired by the concept of the project: Moon, sun, galax. Corresponds to age. With the same font, we changed the outline to create the name of each corresponding course
